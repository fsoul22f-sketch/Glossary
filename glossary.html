consideration /kənˌsɪdəˈreɪʃn/ noun

Careful thought about someone's feelings or needs before acting.

"When is it ever my turn for some consideration?"

scholarship /ˈskɒlərʃɪp/ noun

Money given to a student to help pay for their education, usually based on merit.

"Toby got the scholarship. $2,300."

hot shot /ˈhɒt ʃɒt/ idiom

Someone who acts important or shows off their skill, often mockingly.

"You pull that hot shot stuff around me..."

referee /ˌrɛfəˈriː/ verb

To step in and judge a dispute between two people, usually to keep it fair.

"I will not referee!"
