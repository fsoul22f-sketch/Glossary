consideration /kənˌsɪdəˈreɪʃn/ noun

Careful thought about someone's feelings or needs before acting.

She always shows consideration for her coworkers' time.

ملاحظه، توجه به احساسات یا نیاز دیگران

scholarship /ˈskɒlərʃɪp/ noun

Money given to a student to help pay for their education, usually based on merit.

He got a full scholarship to study engineering.

بورسیه تحصیلی

hot shot /ˈhɒt ʃɒt/ idiom

Someone who acts important or shows off their skill, often mockingly.

Stop acting like such a hot shot in front of the new employees.

آدم خودشیرین یا پرمدعا

referee /ˌrɛfəˈriː/ verb

To step in and judge a dispute between two people, usually to keep it fair.

I'm not going to referee every argument between you two.

داوری کردن، میانجی‌گری کردن

recurring segments /rɪˈkɜːrɪŋ ˈsɛɡmənts/ noun phrase

Fixed, repeating parts of a show or channel that come back regularly, each with its own format or theme.

The podcast has a few recurring segments, like a weekly news roundup.

بخش‌های تکرارشونده و ثابت یک برنامه

memorable /ˈmɛmərəbl/ adjective

Worth remembering; easy to recall because it stands out or leaves a strong impression.

Our trip to the mountains was one of the most memorable weekends of the year.

به‌یادماندنی، فراموش‌نشدنی

doze off /dəʊz ɒf/ phrasal verb

To fall asleep lightly or accidentally, often without meaning to.

He dozed off on the couch while watching TV.
